──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 What happens next
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The eks-agent-platform operator picks up the Platform CR and reconciles
five things in order (see ADR 0004 + docs/architecture.md):

  1. Tenant workload namespace        tenants-{{ .Values.platform.name }}
  2. IRSA role + Bedrock-access policy   /eks-agent-platform/tenants/{{ .Values.platform.name }}/
  3. KMS grant on cmk-data               (per-Platform encryption boundary)
  4. agentgateway routes                 (one per ModelRoute)
  5. kagent Agent + ToolServer CRs       (one per Fleet agent)

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 Verify

  kubectl wait --for=condition=Ready platform/{{ .Values.platform.name }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} --timeout=5m
